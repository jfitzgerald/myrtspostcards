Cover:

The Fallen Monarch, Mariposa Grove, Cal.

Date:

1908

From:

Walter

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams

Palo Alto, Cal., 942 High St.

Message:

Hello Sis, We are at the Wawona Hotel tonight. We are going to see the big trees tomorrow. We made a drive on horse back of about 10 mi and by stage 24 mi today. I get up in the orning before five oclock. It is now [7:?0] Eve. I'm going to bed. Good Night Myrtle, Walter
