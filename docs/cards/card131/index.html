Cover:

New Years card "A Happy New Year

Date:

Wednesday, December 31nd, 1913

Postmark:

Dec 31 8am Honolulu

From:

Will E

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Room 18 Rea Bldg. San Jose, Cal

Message:

SS Houalulau Dec 28/13
Dear Myrtle
-- Am fine and dandy. Spent xmas at sea. Will be in Honolulu for New Years. Had quite a rough trip over so far. Wish you and folks a Happy New Year. Saw Mary P. again told her all about you Write soon Will E
