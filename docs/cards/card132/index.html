Valentine Greetings - Something makes me think of Somebody forcing me Somewhere and I believe if is to You

Date:

Wednesday, February 11nd, 1914

From:

Uncle Frank

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Room 8 Rea Bld. San Jose Calif.

Message:

Dear Myrtle,
Yours here to-day, guess our last letter past each other in the night. [?] I know it as I see yours the day after. ]?] Glad you are having a good time [?] to tie up or tie down either for a few years [?] hope you [?]Guess it runs in the family but be good with love--Uncle Frank
