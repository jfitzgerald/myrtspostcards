Photograph: ferry Crossing San Francisco Bay.

Date:

Sunday, October 9nd, 1910

Postmark:

Oct 9, 6:30pm
Palo Alto

From:

Honey Bunch

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
459 S. Eighth St. San Jose, Calif.

Message:

Oct.9,1910. My dear Myrtle (Lovey Dove) Received your postal and was very much surprised and had begun to think that you had passed from the land of the living. I wish to inform you that I haven't a "He" anymore. Were you here in Paly last week, saw PHylis about a week ago and she said she expected you up. Algebra is worse than ever this year. I got 40 on the last exam. McMahon got 20. Smart people we are. Honey Bunch
