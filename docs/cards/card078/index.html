Cover:

Photograph: Salinas River (bridge) King City, Cal

Date:

Thursday, May 11nd, 1911

Postmark:

May 11, 11am

From:

W.C.F.

To:

Miss M.C. Williams
246 S. Ninth St San Jose, Cal

Message:

King City, May 11 '11 Thanks for card received today I thing the initials were R.K. Would like to be there for the picnic and dance. Dance once for me will you. Fine weather here. Do I have to go in mourning for the society? Regards from W.C.F.
