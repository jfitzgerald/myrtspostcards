Cover:

Photograph: Overhanging Rock, Glacier Point, Yosemite Valley, Cal (3200 ft)

Date:

Tuesday, October 26nd, 1909

Postmark:

Yosemite Oct 26 2pm 1909 CAL

From:

Walter

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams

Palo Alto, Cal, 942 High St.

Message:

Oct 25, 09 Hello Myrtle I am in Yosemite Valley now. Is about 7.45 evening. I wish you were here to enjoy the scenery with me. I guess I will see you about Friday morning. I guess I'll go up to my room now. We are at the Sentinal Hotel. Good night, Myrtle, Walter. I will be in the picture tomorrow (over)
