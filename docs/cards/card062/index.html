Cover:

The "Empress" C.P.R. Hotel, Victoria, British Columbia

Date:

Thursday, November 3nd, 1910

From:

P.C.K.?

To:

Miss M. Williams
246 S. 9th Street, San Jose, Cal

Message:

Dearest,
Don't disappoint me as I am planning on your visit. I am trying to get Mama to go and see you out there in the meantime as I think it would do her good. Please come at noon and if you let me know what train I will surely meet it. If not home to room 8. I am simply growing thin waiting to see you. I want to talk so much. I received another card from W.J. today. How is W.T. Friday noon [?]forever yours, P.C.K.
