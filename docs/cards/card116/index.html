Cover:

A Merry Christmas To You

Date:

Thursday, December 12nd, 1912

From:

Will

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Room 18 Rea Bldg. San Jose Cal

Message:

Dear Myrtle,
12/11/12
Am up in Portland again. I guess I will get my Xmas on board the ship. Hope you have a nice one. I heard Mrs. Desmond's school was going to give a dance. How about it are u going Hoping this reaches you in the best of health. I am sincerely, Will
