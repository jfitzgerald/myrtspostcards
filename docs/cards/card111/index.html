Message:

Bandon Ore 10/8/12
Dear Myrtle
Am still on the same old run. Am going to be home some time after this week. Who is that friend of yours. I know Jay Jones [?] mech. How is Hazel and [?] another Went salmon fishing got a whole bunch of them. Gee the country is great up here. Will c/o 1106 Merch Ex Bldg. S.F. c/o [?]
