Cover:

Hotel Stockton, Stockton, Cal

Date:

Tuesday, January 31nd, 1911

Postmark:

Jan 31, 6pm

From:

JB

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
246 S. 9th st., San Jose, Calif

Message:

June 31, '11 I recd No.1 postal OK. I was just going to write u one when I got yours. Well I hope you got house without drowing. It stopped raining at noon and it is a peach of a day. Will write letter later. Hope to hear from you soon. J.B.
