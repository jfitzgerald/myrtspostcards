Cover:

Truckee Meadows, Tallac, Lake Tahoe, California

Date:

Thursday, September 1nd, 1910

From:

W.C.F.

To:

Miss Myrtle C. Williams

Message:

Priest Valley, Cal, Sept 1, 1910. Your card received. Thanks very much for same. Hooray! Remember me to those three girls. No, on second thought, I guess you had better not. They may think I am too fresh. They would be right though. With best regards, W.C.F.
