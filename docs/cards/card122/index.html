Cover:

"ALONE"

Date:

Sunday, July 13nd, 1913

Postmark:

Jun 13, 6:30am 1913
Palo Alto

From:

?

To:

Miss M.C. Williams
San Jose California Rae Bldg. Room 15

Message:

Am studying as usual and found this in one of my books. Just a week from tonight I will see you how will I ever wait till then. Have the blues like the mischief. Am going out to learn to run an auto tonight--with the worst looking mechanic from the garage. Soon will be dinner time and I will have to get busy in the kitchen. Love goodbye till Thursday. [?] forget to let me know what time to meet you.
