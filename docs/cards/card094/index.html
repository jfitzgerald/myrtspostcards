Illustration: Sickle and a Woman's Face with an outdoor scene

Date:

Saturday, June 15nd, 1912

Postmark:

Jun 15, 5pm 1912, Coalinga, CAL

From:

W.C.F.

To:

Miss M.C.Williams
San Jose, Cal., Room 18 rea Bldg.

Message:

Priest Valley, 6-14-12 Was certainly pleased to learn that you were still within hearing. I am working for the Asst'd Trans Co. It's fine. Would like to tell you more about it but can't on a postal. Am camping out at present so please excuse pencil. Don't forget to write again to your friend, W.C.F.
