Cover:

Illustration: Young man hunting duck. Sept 13, 1910, Best Wishes and Kindest Regards, Me

Date:

Wednesday, September 14nd, 1910

Postmark:

Sept 14, Priest Valley

From:

W.C.F.

To:

Miss Myrtle C. Williams
459 S. Eighth St., San Jose, Cal

Message:

Sept13, Good Luck. Thanks for the card I received today. You are doing fine in typing. It's too bad about Mrs. Holden. I feel sorry for Marian, Poor girl, but I guess we all have to take our turn when our time comes. From your friend, W.C.F.
