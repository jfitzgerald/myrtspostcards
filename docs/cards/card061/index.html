Cover:

Graphic of flowers; California Wild Flowers; [shorthand]

Date:

1910

Postmark:

1910: Nov 2, Priest Valley

From:

W.C.F.Te! He!

To:

Miss Myrtle C. Williams, 246 S. 9th St. San Jose, Cal

Message:

Nov1, '10 Thanks for the pretty flower post card you sent me. What kind of a flower do you call it? I think I saw some with Buffalo Bill's show when I was in S.J. A pretty good picture of me don't you think? Ha! Ha! I just know I would have looked handsome in the rig you described. I wish I could have been there. I would make it "Hurrah" for the "Sheet & Pillow case" I had. I had a fine ten mile horse back ride before sun up yesterday am. It was fine but Oh! wasn't it cold! I should say it was. I had icicles on my mustache. W.C.F. Te! He!
