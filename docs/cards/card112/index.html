Cover:

Photograph: Arrival of Steammer at Santa Cataline Island, Cal

Date:

Friday, November 8nd, 1912

Postmark:

Nov 8 5pm San Francisco CAL

From:

Will

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Rm 18 Rea Bldg San Jose, Cal

Message:

11/6/12
Say Hello 2 Hazel Dear Myrtle,
I awful sorry that I didn't get to see you but as it was I had to hurry. Will see you next time. Am on stmr Yosemite now was at Catalina island yesterday on boat on other side the Hermosa as wireless opr just 4 one trip Will. Write soon
