Mama

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Tulare Tulare Co Calif

Message:

Palo Alto Nov 22, 1909,
Dear Myrtle,
Hazel read your letter this noon, we also read yours written Wed and you should have gotten one from us Sat. You must send a card every day or two to Aunt Lydia. She is not able to walk around [?]. Oh get Hazel and Margaret have gone to town. Will mail you a letter tomorrow, Mama
