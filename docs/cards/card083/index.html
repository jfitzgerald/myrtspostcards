Cover:

Art of woman

Date:

Tuesday, August 1nd, 1911

Postmark:

San Jose, Aug 1, 3pm, 1911

From:

Unk

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Sunnyvale, Cal

Message:

Myrtle dear, don't be angry. I would have wrote to you but I was waiting for Esther--she came Saturday. I am going to have my picnic Sunday and want you and his Honor to come then I will have my party on next Saturday night--Trusting you will make every effort to come. I will see you or call you before then. Lovingly {Margaret?]
