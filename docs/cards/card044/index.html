Cover:

Photograph: General View, Stanford University & Lake

Date:

Sunday, July 10nd, 1910

From:

Mabel

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
459 S. 8th St., San Jose, Calif

Message:

Dear Myrtle,
So glad to hear from you. I was so puzzled because I could not think who sent me that postal until I remembered. I am sorry you are not going to school here anymore. Are you going to High down here when it starts? I went to the skating rink last night. Had a fine time. I can skate now, you know. I thought you would like it down here I know I would. Goodby--Mabel I couldn't write much as it is getting late and I must get to town.
