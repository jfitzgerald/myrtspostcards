Cover:

The Capitol, Washington,D.C.

Date:

Wednesday, December 21nd, 1910

Postmark:

Dec 21, Chicago, ILL

From:

L.M.W

To:

Mrs. Lillian Williams
459 S. Eighth St. San Jose, California

Message:

623 Wellington St., Chigo, Ill, Nov 30, 1910, We spent two months in Va this fall and came home through Richmond Va, Norfok, and Washington. Had a fine time. I'm not very well but hope to be soon. We expect to be in Cal this winter after Holidays and we want to see you all. With love, L.M.W.
