Message:

Santa Rosa, Cal, June 5 1908,
Dear Mrs. Williams of the Girls,
How is your cold Mrs. Williams--and Hazel, how is that dear little Pinkie? Take good care of both (the cold and the cat) Don't forget your promise to write to me, Myrtle. I shall be at home in about two weeks. Santa Rosa is a pretty place, and I am having a very plesant visit. Yours, Etta [?]
