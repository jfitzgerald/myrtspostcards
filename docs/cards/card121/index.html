Cover:

Photograph: City street scene

Date:

Friday, April 11nd, 1913

Postmark:

April 11 2pm Honolulu Hawaii

From:

Will

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
Room 18, Rea Bldg San Jose, Calif U.S.A.

Message:

Stmr China Apr 8, 1913 [Shorthand characters] Am on way to Honolulu, China, Japan and Philippines. Pretty nice out today. Will get back to Frisco about June 10 or so. Well such is life. Last week today I was think seriously of having a life preserver around me and this week 500 miles out from Frisco 4 the Orient. Write soon. Sincerely Will
