Cover:

Illustration of Sweet Peas, Purple

Date:

Tuesday, October 11nd, 1910

Postmark:

Oct 11, San Jose

From:

B.J.

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
San Jose [crossed out], Sunnyvale, Cal

Message:

Hello Myrtle, I thought I would write a few lines. How are you any how. I think my father is going to come over their and buy a ranch, likes the climate better over their, your friend B.J.
