Cover:

A Christmas Dinner

Date:

Friday, January 5nd, 1912

Postmark:

Jan 5, 1912 Tracy, CAL

From:

D. [?]

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams
246 South Ninth St. San Jose Calif

Message:

Dear Myrtle,
I will drop you a card to let you know I have reached as far as Tracy. I am going to Livingston to see the falls will write you a long letter from D. [?] This is a rather late card but I am always late Ho. Ho. Will be home this P.M. 3:30 closing I remain as ever, your true--friend.
