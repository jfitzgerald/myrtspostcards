Photograph: Charter Oaks,<br>Palo Alto, Cal

Date:

Wednesday, August 17nd, 1910

Postmark:

Aug 17, 1910
Palo Alto 5pm

From:

M.P.

To:

Miss Myrtle Williams

Message:

Dear Myrtle,
I excuse you this time for not writing to [?] me but "never again!" No I have never eceived a postal like the one you sent. It is very prettyt. How are you getting along in school? Is Hazel going to school? I hope you don't get two of any postals I send you. Sincerely, M.P. Write again.
