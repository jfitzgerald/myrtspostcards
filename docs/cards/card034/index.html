Cover:

Photograph: 40+ young ladies with hair in Gibson Girl style

Date:

Wednesday, May 19nd, 1909

Postmark:

New Wilmington, May 19, 1909

From:

Frances?

To:

Mr. Chas.H.Williams

Palo Alto, Cal

Message:

Dear Mother,
You did not know I had such a large family did you. Second one from the end of the top row on the left is Clara. I put a spot of ink on her so you could find her. We are all well and busy. Hope you are enjoying the same blessing. Write to me cand tell me what you know, Affectionately, Frances
